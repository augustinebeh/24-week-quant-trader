## Tips for Success

1. **Consistency > Intensity**
   - Better to do 1-2 hours daily than 10 hours once a week
   - Your brain needs time to absorb concepts

2. **Code Everything**
   - Don't just read - type out every example
   - Modify examples to see what breaks
   - Experiment freely

3. **Document Everything**
   - Future you will thank present you
   - Employers will see your documentation skills
   - Writing solidifies learning

4. **Ask "Why?"**
   - Don't just accept what works
   - Understand why it works
   - This depth separates you from others

5. **Build in Public Eventually**
   - Around Week 13, start a blog
   - Share your trading strategies
   - This builds your reputation
